originally made for:
for psx story jam #4

(disclaimer i suffer from psychosis as well as divorce)

# Resources
Hydration, Saturation, but also Creativity, Faith, Science, etc
Could be used as money, or mana, or health.

# Items
Increase Resources with Item
Food increases Saturation, Art supplies increase Creativity, etcetera.

# NPCS (Entity vs Identity)
NPCs can trigger dialogue and exist out of 2 parts:
Entity and Identity. Identity are the qualities of the personality,
Entity can posess any Identity. For example, a Phone can have the Identity of the person you're calling.

# Quests
Quests trigger Dialogue and require resources to complete.
On completion, Quests can trigger an Event

# Events
Events when triggered can toggle any GameObject
Or Teleport any GameObject from point A to point B.
